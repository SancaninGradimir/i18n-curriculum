`The website offers a faster checkout process for returning customers.` - Hapa, `returning customers` ni watu ambao tayari wamefanya ununuzi hapo awali, kwa hivyo hawahitaji kuingiza maelezo yao yote tena.

`The developer asked the team for their thoughts on the new feature.` - Hii ina maana msanidi programu aliomba maoni au mawazo kutoka kwa timu kuhusu kipengele hicho.
