# --explanation--

Kitenzi cha `Present Perfect Continuous` (`have/has been` + kitenzi katika fomu ya `-ing`) kinatumika kuelezea tendo lililoanza zamani na bado linaendelea au lina athari za hivi karibuni. Kwa mfano:

`She has been studying for hours, and she's getting tired.` - To znači da je počeo da uči pre mnogo vremena i i dalje uči sada, a postoje vidljivi efekti.

Wakati James anasema `We've already been putting in extra hours`, anamaanisha kuwa timu ilianza kufanya kazi kwa saa za ziada muda uliopita na bado wanaendelea kufanya hivyo sasa. Mfano mwingine:

`She has been studying for hours, and she's getting tired.` - Ovo znači da je počela/počeo da čita davno i još uvek čita, i postoje vidljivi efekti.
