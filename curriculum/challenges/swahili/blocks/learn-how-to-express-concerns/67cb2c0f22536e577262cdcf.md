# --explanation--

`Could you...` ni njia ya heshima kuanza ombi kwa mtu. `I'll...` hutumika katika jibu kuashiria ahadi au kujitolea kufanya jambo fulani. Kwa mfano:

- **Lily:** `Could you send me the report by tomorrow?` - Mtu huyu anaomba ripoti itolewe siku inayofuata.  

- **Noah:** `Sure. I'll email it to you in the morning.` - Mtu huyu anaahidi kutuma ripoti kwa barua pepe.
