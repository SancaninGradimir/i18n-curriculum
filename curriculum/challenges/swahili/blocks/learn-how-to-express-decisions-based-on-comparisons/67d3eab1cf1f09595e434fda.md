# --explanation--

`However` Koristi se za uvođenje razlike ili drugačije ideje/koncepta. Primer:

`This laptop is powerful. However, it is also very expensive.` - Hii ina maana kompyuta mpakato ni nzuri, lakini kuna upande mbaya - ni ghali.  

`Bonuses` ni malipo ya ziada yanayotolewa kwa wafanyakazi kama zawadi kwa kazi nzuri. Kwa mfano:  

`The company gives yearly bonuses based on performance.` - Hii ina maana wafanyakazi wanapokea pesa za ziada kwa kufanya vizuri.  

`Stock` inahusu hisa katika kampuni ambazo wafanyakazi wanaweza kumiliki. Kwa mfano:  

`Many startups offer stock options as part of employee compensation.` - Ovo znači da zaposleni primaju akcije u kompaniji zajedno sa svojom platom.
