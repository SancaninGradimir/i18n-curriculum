# --explanation--  

`Introduce` To znači pomoći nekome da upozna novu osobu. Na primer:

`She introduced me to her manager at the meeting.` - Ovo znači da ti je neko pomogao da se sastaneš sa njegovim menadžerom prvi put.

`A few` linamaanisha idadi ndogo, zaidi ya moja lakini si nyingi. Kwa mfano:  

`I have a few questions about the project.` - Hii inamaanisha una maswali machache.  

`You'd like` ni njia ya heshima kusema "kama unataka". Kwa mfano:  

`I can help you with your work if you'd like.` - Hii inamaanisha unatoa msaada ikiwa mtu anataka.
