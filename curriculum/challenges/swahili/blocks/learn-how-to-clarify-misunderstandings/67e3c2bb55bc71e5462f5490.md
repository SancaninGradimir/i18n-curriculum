# --explanation--

`Mentioned` to znači kratko pomenuti nešto ili o tome govoriti. Često se koristi kada neko preuzima temu u razgovoru. Na primer:

`She mentioned the meeting time in her email.` – Alirejelea wakati wa mkutano.

`Polymorphism` ni dhana ya programu. Inamaanisha kitendakazi, njia, au operator moja inaweza kutumika na aina tofauti za data au vitu. Kwa mfano:

`In object-oriented programming, polymorphism allows a single method to behave differently based on the object that calls it.` - Hapa, `polymorphism` inarejelea njia inayoweza kufanya vitendo tofauti kulingana na ni kitu gani kinaitumia. Kwa mfano, njia ile ile `draw()` inaweza kufanya tofauti inapoitwa na kitu cha `Circle` au kitu cha `Rectangle`.
